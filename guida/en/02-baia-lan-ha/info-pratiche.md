## Practical Info — Lan Ha Bay

### Getting There

From Ninh Binh, your previous stop, you reach Cat Ba (the gateway to Lan Ha Bay) by 
bus + ferry/speedboat in about 3.5-4 hours. The connection often passes through Hai Phong, from where the ferries to the island depart
. Once aboard the cruise, you sleep overnight in a cabin on the bay.

**Recommended Companies:**
- **Cat Ba Express** - 
Considered the best option, with connections from Ninh Binh/Hai Phong
 ([official site](https://catbaexpress.com))
- **Good Morning Cat Ba** - 
Daily combined bus + boat service
 ([site](https://goodmorningcatba.com))

**Transport Details:**
- **Duration**: 
3.5-4 hours total from Ninh Binh

- **Prices**: 
around €15-20 per person for the combined bus + ferry leg

- **Sea leg**: 
speedboat/ferry to Cat Ba, then local bus to Cat Ba Town (30-45 minutes)


**How it works:**
1. Bus from Ninh Binh heading towards Hai Phong / boarding point
2. 
Ferry or speedboat to Cat Ba Island

3. 
Local bus to Cat Ba Town, then transfer to the pier for the cruise


💡 **Insider tip**: 
Book the combined transfer through your Ninh Binh accommodation or the cruise company: avoid awkward changes with your luggage


**Departing — towards Hanoi:**

From Cat Ba, once the cruise is over, you head back to Hanoi on the direct bus (bus + ferry + bus) in about 3-4 hours. Frequent departures in the morning arriving in the Old Quarter; many companies (Cat Ba Express, Good Morning Cat Ba) offer pickup from the pier.


### Getting Around

Cat Ba Island is small and easy to explore. Here are the transport options:

**Motorbikes (scooters) - Recommended**
- **Price**: 
150,000 VND/day (automatic scooter), 200,000 VND (manual)
 = around €6-8/day
- **Where to rent**: 
Reliable shops everywhere in town
, hotels often include the service
- **Documents**: 
International driving licence recommended but not always required


⚠️ **IMPORTANT**: 
ALWAYS wear a helmet! The police carry out frequent checks in town


**Motorbike taxi (xe ôm)**
- **Price**: 
30,000-100,000 VND per trip
 (1.5-4€)
- **Where to find it**: 
Everywhere in town, on street corners


**Car taxi**
- **Example price**: 
50,000 VND from Cat Ba Town to Cai Beo Dock

- **Note**: 
Beware of covered meters - always negotiate the price


**Grab**
- 
Only works occasionally
 on Cat Ba - don't rely on it

**On foot**
- 
Cat Ba Town can be walked across in 15 minutes

- 
Cat Co 1, 2, 3 and Tung Thu beaches are reachable on foot

### Connectivity & SIM

**Network coverage on Cat Ba:**
- 
Coverage is generally good but can drop slightly during boat tours. Viettel offers the best performance

- 
VinaPhone is particularly reliable in the main resort areas such as Cat Ba


**Best operators for Cat Ba:**
1. **Viettel** - 
Widest coverage, even on islands and remote areas

2. **VinaPhone** - 
Great for 5G speed, ranked second in the world for 5G speed in 2025

3. **MobiFone** - 
A good budget-friendly option, performs well in coastal areas


**Prices and tourist plans:**
- **SIM cost**: 
50,000-100,000 VND (2-4€)

- **Data plans**: 
70,000-100,000 VND for 7 days, 150,000-200,000 VND for 30 days (3-8€)


**Where to buy:**
- At Hanoi airport before departure
- Official operator shops in Cat Ba Town
- 
Passport required for registration (mandatory under Vietnamese law)


**WiFi:**

WiFi is widely available and generally free in Vietnam

- Available in all recommended hotels and restaurants

### Safety

**General situation:**
Cat Ba is generally very safe for tourists, but follow these precautions:

**Road safety (priority #1):**
- 
ALWAYS wear a helmet - frequent police checks in town

- 
Never drive under the influence of alcohol, and don't speed

- 
Respect local rules - disrespectful behaviour by foreigners is damaging relations


**Common scams to avoid:**
- 
Taxis with covered meters - always negotiate the price first

- Inflated prices for tourists - always ask the price first

**Vietnam emergency numbers:**
- **Police**: 
113

- **Fire Brigade**: 
114

- **Ambulance/Medical emergencies**: 
115

- **Search and rescue**: 
112

- **Cat Ba Police**: 
0225.3888.114

- **Cat Ba Medical Centre**: 
0225.3887.237


**Specific safety tips:**
- 
Download offline maps of Cat Ba before you leave

- Always keep a copy of your passport
- 
In case of a language barrier, ask locals or hotel staff for help

- 
The emergency numbers operate only in Vietnamese - bring a translator when reporting crimes


⚠️ **Typhoon season**: If you travel July-September, 
the early warning systems provide 3-5 days' notice
