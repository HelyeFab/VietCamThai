## Practical Info — Ninh Binh

### Getting There

**From Phong Nha to Ninh Binh by night bus (8–10 hours)**


From Phong Nha, your previous stop, Ninh Binh is reached by overnight sleeper buses that cost about €12–15 per person and take 8–10 hours
. 
Departure is typically in the evening (19:30–21:00) with arrival at dawn, so you gain a night and arrive rested
.

**Recommended companies:**
- **Queen Cafe / Full Moon**: 
Sleeper buses with reclining seats, power outlets and a toilet; evening departure from Phong Nha
- **Hung Thanh / The Sinh Tourist**: 
Reliable overnight connections along the Phong Nha–Ninh Binh corridor
- 
Alternatively, the train from Dong Hoi (45 km from Phong Nha) to Ninh Binh — more comfortable but with a transfer and a similar duration


**Where to buy:**
- Online at [Vexere.com](https://vexere.com) - Vietnam's most reliable platform
- [12go.asia](https://12go.asia) to compare prices and schedules for the night buses
- 
Ask your Phong Nha accommodation to book: they often get better prices and pickup in town


**Tips:**
- 
Book in advance to secure a lower berth (more stable)
- 
Bring an extra layer: the night buses keep the air conditioning cranked high
- 
Ninh Binh's bus station is near the train station, beyond the bridge toward Phat Diem


**Departing — toward Lan Ha Bay:**

From the Ninh Binh / Hai Phong area, the next stop is Lan Ha Bay (Cat Ba Island). It's reached by bus + ferry/speedboat in about 3.5–4 hours; many places to stay arrange the combined transfer or the connection via Hai Phong.


### Getting Around

**Grab (taxi app)**

Since June 19, 2024, Grab has been operating in Ninh Binh
, but with limitations:
- **Availability**: 
Mainly in the city centre and near the station, wait times of 10–15 minutes
- **GrabCar**: 
Fixed fares in the app, payment by cash or card
- **GrabBike**: 
About 25,000 VND for short trips, only one passenger per bike
- **Prices**: 
Station→Tam Coc: 70–100k VND with GrabCar, 30–50k VND with GrabBike


**Traditional taxis**

Fair price from 12,000 VND (~€0.5) per km
. **Reliable companies**: 
Mai Linh and Vinasun, with new vehicles and sealed meters
.

**Bicycles**
Perfect for exploring Ninh Binh! **Costs**: about €3/day
- **Ninh Binh Local Tour**: 
Hotline 0986.263.917 or 0962.520.224, delivery in 10–15 minutes
- **Booking**: 
Via Facebook "Ninh Binh Local Tour" or by calling directly


**Motorbike rental**

Cost 100–200k VND (4–8 USD) per day depending on the type
:
- 
Semi-automatic: 100–120k VND/day
- 
Automatic (scooter): 120–150k VND/day


**Recommended agencies**:
- **MOTOGO**: 
New vehicles only, no deposit if you have hotel/flight bookings
- **Huong Duong**: 
City centre, free door-to-door delivery


### Where to Stay

For your budget of **€22/night for a double room**, here are the best options:

#### 1. **Tam Coc Lotus Flower Homestay** 

The best choice in Tam Coc: spotlessly clean rooms, a delicious free breakfast, incredibly helpful owners

- **Price**: ~€20–25/night double
- **Link**: Search on Booking.com for "Tam Coc Lotus Flower"

#### 2. **Tam Coc Golden Sky Homestay**

In the heart of the Tam Coc–Bich Dong tourist area, convenient for getting around

- **Price**: ~€18–22/night double
- **Location**: 
0.5km from Bich Dong, 0.1km from the Thung Nang boat station


#### 3. **Trang An River View Homestay**

Right on the river, surrounded by rice paddies and mountains, breakfast included, friendly staff

- **Price**: ~€15–20/night double
- **Perfect**: For those seeking tranquility and a breathtaking view

#### 4. **New Queen Hotel (Ninh Binh city)**

Hotel with A/C and comfortable beds, free transport to/from the bus station

- **Price**: ~€12–18/night double
- **Advantage**: 
Free shuttle from the station


**Where to stay**:
- **Tam Coc**: 
More convenient, plenty of food options, sociable atmosphere
- **Trang An**: 
More peaceful, fewer tourists, family-run homestays


### Connectivity & SIM

**Recommended operators for Ninh Binh:**

Viettel offers the best performance, prices and internet stability for travellers in 2025
. 
Viettel or Vinaphone offer the best value for speed, coverage and reliability
.

**Network performance**:
- **Viettel**: 
Vietnam's largest provider with extensive coverage, reaching even remote mountainous areas
- **Vinaphone**: 
Good performance in cities but weaker in rural and mountainous areas
- ⚠️ **Avoid Vietnamobile**: 
Not recommended for areas like Ninh Binh


**Where to buy**:
- **Noi Bai Airport** (Hanoi): Official provider stands
- **Official stores** in the city: Cheaper than the airport
- **Online**: 
The Viettel eSIM with a 4GB/day plan is the superior choice for backpackers in 2025


**WiFi**: 
WiFi in hotels is good, but WiFi in caves, mountains, pagodas or rice paddies is not reliable. A strong local SIM is necessary
.

### Safety


Ninh Binh is one of the safest places to visit in Southeast Asia. While small scams exist, serious crime is rare
.

**General safety level:**
- 
Low crime rate: violent crime against foreigners is almost non-existent
- 
Friendly locals, safe for families and solo travellers, including women


**Common scams to avoid:**

1. **Boat tour scams**:
   - 
Unofficial vendors may offer "tickets" at inflated prices outside the attractions
   - 
Buy only at the official counters; a tip to the rowers of 20–50k VND is enough


2. **Taxi scams**:
   - 
Risk of illegitimate taxis (Xe Dù) that refuse to use the meter
   - 
Use reliable services such as Mai Linh and Vinasun, and make sure the meter is running


3. **Online bookings**:
   - 
Ninh Binh authorities issued warnings after a woman lost over $40,000 in a travel booking scam
   - **Tip**: Always book through official sites or recognised agencies

**Vietnam emergency numbers:**
- **Police**: 113
- **Fire brigade**: 114  
- **Medical emergencies**: 115
- **Tourist emergency**: 1800 1779

**Practical tips**:
- 
Vietnamese culture values courtesy. A firm but polite "No" usually works
- 
Keep small change (10–50k VND) to ease transactions and avoid overcharging
- 
Ninh Binh is particularly safe for women travelling alone
