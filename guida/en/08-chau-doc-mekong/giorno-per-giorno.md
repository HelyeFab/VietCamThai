## Day 13 — October 13, 2026 — Among the lotus flowers of the enchanted forest

### Morning

![The Tra Su Forest](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cb/R%E1%BB%ABng_Tr%C3%A0_S%C6%B0%2C_van_giao%2CTinhBi%C3%AAn_%2C_chau_doc%2CAn_Giang_vietnam%2C05-10-2013-Dyt_-_panoramio.jpg/1280px-R%E1%BB%ABng_Tr%C3%A0_S%C6%B0%2C_van_giao%2CTinhBi%C3%AAn_%2C_chau_doc%2CAn_Giang_vietnam%2C05-10-2013-Dyt_-_panoramio.jpg)

Your awakening in charming Chau Doc begins with one of the most magical experiences of the Mekong Delta:
a visit to the Tra Su Forest, particularly beautiful during the rainy season from September to November, when the surface of the water is completely covered by the green of the duckweed
.

**Departure and arrival (8:00-9:30)**

The forest is about 20km from Chau Doc. You can book an organized tour directly from Chau Doc or rent a motorbike to reach it on your own
. Organized tours depart at 8:00 or 14:00 and include transport, guide and entrance ticket.

**The boat experience (9:30-12:30)**

The entrance price is around 100,000-190,000 VND per person (€4-8)
.
First you board a motorboat for 20 minutes through the flooded forest, then switch to the traditional rowed sampans to explore the narrower, more intimate canals
.

In the forest you'll find over 140 plant species and 70 bird species, including storks, herons and cormorants
.
After the boat trip, you'll climb a 25-meter-high observation tower to admire the panoramic view of the forest dotted with hundreds of storks
.

**Useful link**: [Official Tra Su tours website](https://vemekong.com/tra-su-forest-tour-from-chau-doc/)

### Afternoon

**Option A: Relaxation and local exploration**

![Crossing the Mekong](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/a5/Seasonal_flooding_in_Thailand_and_Cambodia_2002_October_9.jpg)

The forest tours last about 3-4 hours, so you'll be back in Chau Doc in the early afternoon
. Take advantage of this to explore central Chau Doc on foot or by bicycle, visiting the local market and the streets along the river.

**Option B (Recommended alternative): Sam Mountain at sunset**

From central Chau Doc, you can take a taxi for 15 minutes (around 50,000-100,000 VND) to the base of Sam Mountain
.
The mountain is 284 meters high and about 6 km from the town center
.

**Temples and cable car (15:00-17:30)**

Visit the Ba Chua Xu Temple at the base, famous for its lotus-flower-shaped architecture with three floors and roofs covered in blue tiles
.
The cable car costs 90,000 VND round trip for adults (60,000 VND one way)
.

### Evening

**Sunset from Sam Mountain (17:30-18:30)**

There's nothing more beautiful than the sunset seen from the top of Sam Mountain: from up there you'll have a panoramic view of the peaceful town of Chau Doc as the sun slowly sinks like a ball of fire
.
Being the highest peak in the Mekong Delta, it offers an unrivaled view over endless rice paddies and winding rivers
.

**Authentic dinner (19:00-21:00)**
For dinner, head to one of these highly recommended restaurants:

- **Quán Mekong** (Le Loi Street):
A popular restaurant set in a quiet courtyard opposite an old colonial villa. Despite the simple plastic chairs and tables, the atmosphere is welcoming
. Specialties: braised fish and sweet-and-sour soup.

- **Bassac Restaurant** (Victoria Chau Doc Hotel):
Elegant restaurant with a river-view terrace, perfect for enjoying an aperitif at sunset. It offers Vietnamese and international cuisine
.

- **Hội Quán Châu Đốc**:
Near the Sam Mountain temples, famous for its terracotta dishes and affordable prices. Highly recommended are the sweet-and-sour fish soup (canh ca chua) and the braised fish
.

### Tips of the Day

**🚌 Transport**:
The bus journey from Ho Chi Minh City to Chau Doc costs $7-10 and takes 5-6 hours. Reliable companies: Phuong Trang and Futa Bus
. Book in advance for the best seats!

**🏨 Accommodation**:
Budget hotels from $11-25/night: Paris Hotel ($25), Little Saigon Hotel ($15), The Luxe Hotel Chau Doc ($21)
. For maximum comfort: Victoria Chau Doc Hotel ($93).

**💡 Insider tips**:
-
Tra Su is at its most splendid from October 2025 onwards
 — you're in the perfect period!
-
Dress appropriately for the temples: avoid shorts and sleeveless shirts

-
Bring insect repellent if you visit during the rainy months

-
The best time for Sam Mountain is the dry season (December-April), but October is fine. Avoid weekends for smaller crowds

.
